Matthew Bryan | Senior Lecturer University of Central Florida

I’m currently a senior lecturer in the Department of Writing and Rhetoric at the University of Central Florida, where I also serve as assistant director of the University Writing Center.

My training is in creative writing, and I still write both essays and stories. My research has been focused in the areas of writing program administration and writing center studies. You can check out my latest work at my Projects page.

Please reach out if you’re interested in collaborating!
